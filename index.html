About OreVision

OreVision pioneers AI-powered ore sorting solutions, revolutionizing mining in Egypt and beyond. Our technology enhances efficiency, precision, and sustainability.

Founded in 2025 by Mohammed Madany, we focus on empowering SMEs with affordable, scalable sorting services.

Our Technology

Our AI-driven system uses artificial intelligence to sort ores/minerals like feldspar and quartz in real-time, ensuring high accuracy.

Scalable and adaptable, our technology reduces costs and environmental impact, tailored for MENA’s mining challenges.

How It Works

  1. Image Capture: Industrial cameras scan ores on a conveyor.
  2. AI Analysis: Deep learning identifies valuable minerals.
  3. Automated Sorting: Robots separate ores from waste.
